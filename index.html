Help guide
my closet
Your personal wardrobe, outfit builder,
and style assistant — all in one place.
🗺 Getting around
The bottom bar has six tabs. Tap any icon to navigate.
Closet
Browse, search, and filter all your items
Add
Add a new clothing item to your wardrobe
Outfits
Build, save, and browse outfit history
Suggest
Weather-based outfit ideas and gap analysis
More
Color palette, packing list, wishlist, archive
Stats
Wardrobe overview and backup tools
➕ Adding items
Tap the + button (bottom nav or the floating purple button on the Closet screen) to add a new item.
Fill in as many or as few fields as you like. At minimum, just a name and category is enough to get started.
1Add up to multiple photos by tapping the + photo area
2Enter the item name and brand (brand autocompletes from your history)
3Pick a category — size options update automatically to match
4Select a color, size, seasons, and fit status
5Add tags like "activewear" or "formal" — create your own too
6Tap Add to closet — or Cancel to go back without saving
👗 Managing items
Tap any item in your Closet to open the action sheet with these options:
Edit
Change any detail — photos, name, size, fit, tags, and more
Duplicate
Copy an item — useful for the same piece in different colors
Laundry
Flag it as in the wash — hides it from outfit suggestions
Archive
Move to donate/storage pile — not deleted, easily restored
Delete
Permanently remove the item — this cannot be undone
🔍 Search & filter
Use the search bar at the top of your Closet to find items by name, brand, or notes.
The filter chips let you browse by category, season, or fit status. The status dropdown lets you switch between your active closet, laundry pile, archived items, or everything at once.
The sort dropdown lets you arrange items by recently added, name, brand, category, or color.
⭐ Outfits
The Outfits tab has three views — Builder, Saved, and History.
1In Builder, tap any slot (Top, Bottom, Shoes etc.) to pick from your closet
2Give your outfit a name in the field at the bottom
3Tap Save outfit — it appears in Saved and History
4In Saved, tap Load to bring an outfit back into the builder
5Tap Style tips ↗ to get AI color coordination advice
Items that are in laundry or archived are automatically hidden from the outfit picker.
💡 Smart suggest
The Suggest tab pulls live weather for San Jose and recommends an outfit from your wearable items.
1Choose a season override if you want (otherwise it auto-detects from weather)
2Pick an occasion — Casual, Work, Date night, Activewear, and more
3Tap Suggest an outfit — a random wearable combination appears
4Tap Style tips ↗ for AI advice on the suggested look
Only items marked "Fits well" that are not in laundry or archived will appear in suggestions.
📊 Gap analysis
Scroll down on the Suggest tab to see Gap analysis. It automatically scans your wardrobe and flags:
Low stock
Categories with fewer than 2 wearable items
Thin seasons
Seasons with fewer than 3 items
Tap Get AI shopping advice ↗ for specific item, style, and color recommendations to fill those gaps.
🎨 Color palette
Go to More → Palette to see your entire wardrobe organized by color as visual swatches. Each swatch shows the color name and how many items you own in that color.
Tap any color swatch to jump to your Closet filtered to show only that color — great for spotting gaps or planning outfits.
🧳 Packing list
Go to More → Packing to build a smart packing list from your actual wardrobe.
1Enter your destination or occasion (e.g. "Beach vacation")
2Enter the number of days
3Pick the season for your destination
4Tap Build packing list — it pulls real items from your closet
5Tap Packing advice ↗ for AI tips on combinations and what to add
Only wearable, non-laundry, non-archived items are included in your packing list.
🛍 Shopping wishlist
Go to More → Wishlist to track items you want to buy, organized by priority.
1Type an item name and optional price
2Set priority — High, Medium, or Low
3Tap Add — items sort automatically by priority
4Tap the circle to check off items you've bought
5Tap × to remove an item
After buying something, add it to your closet inventory too!
📦 Archive & donate
Archiving moves an item out of your active closet without deleting it permanently — perfect for seasonal items, pieces you're donating, or things in storage.
1Tap any item → Archive / donate in the action sheet
2Find all archived items under More → Archive
3Tap Restore to bring an item back to your active closet
4Tap Remove to delete permanently
🧺 Laundry status
Mark items as in the wash so they don't appear in outfit suggestions or the outfit picker until they're clean again.
1Tap any item → Mark as in laundry
2The item gets a 🧺 badge and disappears from suggestions
3Tap the item again → Mark as clean to restore it
4Use the status dropdown in Closet to see all laundry items
💾 Backup & restore
Your wardrobe data is stored in Safari's local storage on your device. Back it up regularly so you never lose your data.
1Go to Stats → scroll to Backup & restore
2Tap Export wardrobe backup to download a .json file
3Save it to iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or email it to yourself
4To restore: tap Import from backup file and pick your saved file
Back up after any big wardrobe session — especially before clearing your browser or getting a new phone!
✨ Pro tips
🏷
Use tags like "activewear", "formal", or "vacation" to group items across categories for quick filtering
📸
Add multiple photos per item — front, back, and detail shots help you remember exactly what you own
🎯
Mark goal items (things that don't fit yet but you're working toward) — they stay visible but are excluded from suggestions
🌸
Always open the app from your home screen icon in Safari to make sure your data stays consistent
